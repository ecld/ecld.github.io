The International Workshop on Early Communication and Language Development is a two-and-a-half-day event hosted by the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid (UAM, Spain). This workshop will bring together leading European research groups and international experts specializing in early communicative and linguistic development. The event aims to foster meaningful collaboration among established teams, facilitate research networking, and explore technological innovations—including the integration of artificial intelligence into studies of early developmental processes.

More information about the ECLD—including the speakers, program, and other relevant details—will be available soon. Stay tuned for updates!

Financial support for this event was provided by UAM’s program, 'Ayudas para la organización de Jornadas Científicas del Programa Propio de Investigación'
